# Deatheless Traveler

Uploaded some stuff in 'Pictures' that is related to a project from 6 years ago.

![Image](Content/Images/deathless.jpg)

Usually I would try to do manga/comics, but for some reason I wasn't really able to go for it and every attempt would turn me around.
So one of the things I decided to try is to have a text based stories with an image supporting them.
I'm providing enough visuals to transfer the vibe and the rest is done in a text form.

Because my main problem with books was lack of the visual 'vibe' and with comics is lack of any type of poetry.

Funny enough, as usual it lead me to nowhere, but now I have a set of images just laying around and I can talk about them now, 6 years later)

```
Images were created by traveling the world in Microsoft Flight Simulator
making screenshots
painting things on top
those are real places with real metric data
```

![Image](Content/Images/deathless_traveler.jpg)

The idea is pretty much: 'If Frieren was depressing'. Even though it is before Frieren was a thing.
We have a female protagonist who is just traveling the world overrun by machines, that kind-of enslaved humanity.
Humans do not even see the machines that are built all over the world, only the lady can see and sense them all.

In Africa she finds a small broken robot, gives him a fix and travel across the world, just vibing it out and seeing beauty in 'depression'.
She charges her small companion by using bigger robots, who do not mind the lady, because she causes no harm and just observes.
She meets different robots who became conscious and just learns more about the world, 'entities' and humans with complete detachment.

Overall just a vibe) Beauty of despair. Also shown by visual bright red umbrella of a lady.
