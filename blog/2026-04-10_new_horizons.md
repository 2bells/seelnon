## Why Markdown?
It's much easier to write than raw HTML. You can use:
- **Bold** text
- *Italic* text
- [Links](https://google.com)

### Videos
To include a YouTube video, just add a line like this anywhere in your file:
[video: dQw4w9WgXcQ]

(Replace `dQw4w9WgXcQ` with any YouTube video ID!)
