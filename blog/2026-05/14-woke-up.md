# Tetri-Shot

Main idea is... you 'shoot' your piece instead of it falling down. If you slow, it falls down and you can't control it at all.

![image](Content/Projects/Tetri-Shot/tetri-shot_thmb.jpg)


## Why is it peak? 

Because you can shoot a piece above first and then below, making 1st piece to fall on the 2nd one, creating a 'setplay' mid air... 

It feels really good to pull off. 

And you can create 'holes' of specific pieces and 'shoot' into the prepared 'hole' later. AND no hypertapping problems, etc, because you aiming with your mouse.
