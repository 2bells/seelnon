Have you ever smelled pancakes half a globe away? It is a better smell than cigarettes.

```
thought of a day:

if senses are the easiest
thing to trick,
observed reality is
a sleight of hand performance.
>_

fun part:
who is the magician?
```

- Movies are also just a slideshow that moves 'fast enough', yet we feel.
- Games are also just a bunch of gates that open 'fast enough', yet we care.
- Paintings are also just a bunch of strokes that 'describe enough', yet we see.
- Stories are also just a bunch of symbols that 'describe enough', yet we imagine.

All we need is 'just enough', so we can experience.
