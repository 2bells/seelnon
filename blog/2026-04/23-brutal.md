## <div style="color: #000000; font-family: VT323;">**CONCEPT BRUTE!**</div>

![Image](Content/Projects/Concept_Brute/img/showcase_brute_concept_thmb_2.jpg)

Main ideas here are: color palette that combines your main colors, so you can just set up 6 colors and other will be 'generated for you' based on values/hues... it is peak digital color mixing and not utilised well enough.

![Image](Content/Projects/Concept_Brute/img/concept_thmb.jpg)

Then it is control of values/hues with pressure/speed. 

Lately I was more interested in speed control, rather than pressure sensitivity of a pen. Because it makes sense on paper, but speed of a stroke usually has more influence than 'pressure' and for 'web' Windows Ink is not that good.

![Image](Content/Projects/Concept_Brute/img/speed_controls_thmb.jpg)
