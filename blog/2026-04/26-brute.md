## ANYWAY!

She is now a mascot of the APP, because why not. So there were updates to 'Concept Brute'... previous save is gone. But there are:

- **Palette Extractions:** Steals colors from your PNG.
- **Patterns:** So you can have lines and stuff.
- **Projects:** Many of them: database storage.
- **Export:** Save and be happy
- **Chunks/WEBP:** Can adjust quality of canvas per project!
- **DATA:** Shows you how much MB you have in you.
- **INFO:** So you can see hotkeys fro the app
- **IMG REF:** Some stuff you can do with images (some buttons don't work yet)
